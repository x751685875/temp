We considered the value of this course from many aspects, and finally summarized it for you from two aspects: skills and knowledge, and the people who benefit from it: (Please note that our content is optimized through artificial intelligence tools and carefully reviewed by our editorial staff.) What skills and knowledge will you acquire during this course? Digital Marketing Strategies: Students will gain a strong foundation in digital marketing strategies, enabling them to create effective online marketing campaigns. Customer Value Journey: They will learn the concept of the customer value journey, understanding how to lead customers through a structured path from awareness to conversion. Sales Funnel Navigation: Learners will acquire the skills to effectively move potential customers through a sales funnel, optimizing the conversion process. Customer Profiling: The course will teach students how to create detailed customer profiles for digital marketing, allowing for more targeted and personalized campaigns. Fundamentals of Marketing: They will grasp the fundamentals of marketing, both in the traditional and digital contexts, providing a solid knowledge base for their marketing efforts. Who will benefit from this course? This course will benefit non-technical sales professionals, single business owners, and marketers who are looking to understand and navigate the complex world of digital marketing. It is designed to help them learn customer persona and customer value journey, as well as how to move customers through the sales funnel. By understanding the fundamentals of marketing and digital marketing, participants will be better equipped to successfully run their businesses.

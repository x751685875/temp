Skills and Knowledge Acquisition:

1. Agile Methodologies: Gain an understanding of Agile principles and its significance in modern project management.
2. What is Scrum?: Explore the fundamental concepts and components of the Scrum framework.
3. Roles in Scrum: Learn about the key roles in Scrum, including Scrum Master, Product Owner, and Development Team.
4. Sprints: Understand the concept of Sprints in Scrum, time-boxed iterations that drive project progress.
5. Stories: Explore the role of User Stories in Scrum, a valuable tool for defining project requirements and user needs.

- Agile Competence: Develop a foundational understanding of Agile methodologies and their application.

- Effective Collaboration: Enhance collaboration skills through a shared understanding of Scrum practices.
- Adaptive Project Management: Be prepared to implement Agile and Scrum practices in real-world projects.
- Career Advancement: Stand out as a professional with Agile and Scrum expertise.
- Team Productivity: Contribute to improved team productivity and project delivery.

- Efficiency: Learn how to optimize project processes and workflows using Scrum techniques.

Suitability for Preparing Further Education:

- Team Collaboration Skills: Relevant for individuals aiming to strengthen their collaboration and teamwork abilities.
- Introduction to Scrum: Ideal for beginners considering a career in Scrum or Agile project management.

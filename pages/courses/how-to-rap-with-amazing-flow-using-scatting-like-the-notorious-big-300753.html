What skills and knowledge will you acquire during this course? During this course, you will acquire the following skills and knowledge: 1. Understanding and implementing scatting techniques: You will learn what scatting is and how to use it effectively in your rap flow. Scatting is a vocal improvisation technique commonly used in jazz music, and it can greatly enhance your rap skills. 2. Developing a natural rap flow: By practicing scatting exercises over beats, you will learn how to find your own unique and amazing rap flow. This will help you break free from being too focused on lyrics and prioritize flow, resulting in a more polished and professional rap style. 3. Improving rhythm and timing: One of the key aspects of rap is staying on beat and rapping with perfect timing. Through the exercises in this course, you will develop a better sense of rhythm and learn how to rap on beat consistently. 4. Enhancing freestyle abilities: Scatting exercises are not only beneficial for writing hit rap songs but also for improving your freestyle skills. You will learn how to think on your feet and come up with creative and engaging rap verses spontaneously. 5. Gaining a broader musical understanding: The scatting exercises taught in this course are not limited to rap. They are used by songwriters in various genres to write hit songs. By practicing these exercises, you will gain a deeper understanding of music and broaden your musical abilities. Overall, this course will help you become a better rapper by focusing on flow, rhythm, and improvisation through scatting exercises. You will develop a natural rap flow, improve your timing, and enhance your freestyle abilities, ultimately taking your rap skills to the next level. Who will benefit from this course? This course will benefit aspiring rappers who want to improve their rap flow and learn how to rap on beat perfectly. It is particularly useful for beginners who often prioritize lyrics over flow and struggle to find their natural rhythm. By learning scatting techniques used by The Notorious B.I.G. and other successful rappers, participants will be able to develop their own amazing musical rap flow. Additionally, songwriters from various genres can also benefit from the scatting exercises taught in this course to write hit songs.
